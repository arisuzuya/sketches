Sketch Descriptions

Sketch 1: Luna from Sailor Moon

This sketch features Luna, the iconic black cat from Sailor Moon. The drawing captures Luna’s distinct crescent moon marking on her forehead, large expressive eyes, and elegant feline posture. The design emphasizes soft shading to highlight her fur texture, making her appear both mysterious and adorable.

Sketch 2: Totoro

This sketch depicts Totoro, the beloved forest spirit from *My Neighbor Totoro*. The drawing captures Totoro’s round, fluffy body, large eyes, and signature leaf on his head. The style focuses on a soft, welcoming aesthetic, reflecting Totoro’s warm and friendly nature.
